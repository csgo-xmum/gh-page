Conservation Strategy

Human Being Factor

Wild animals have their value, like edible value, medical value, biological research value and so on. For interests and desire, hunters raise shotguns, aim at the wild animals. The extinction of dodo and sharp decrease of Tibetan antelope show obviously that how huge the influence brought by irrational hunting of human beings.

Environment factor

The development of factory industry and the extension of city not only destroy the habitats of wild animals but increase the temporary around the world. A large number of populations seriously affect the life of wild animals.

Do not Buy and Sell

Control from source. Control the market can initially decrease the desire.

Protect Habitat

Protect the area of habitats. Reducing populations to protect resources. Keep the population in K/2(K is environment capacity, the most number in the area).

Establish the Nature Reserve

It is the most efficient way to protect wildlife and is useful to research the biodiversity.

Legislation to Protect

Perfecting and publicity the related laws can increase the sense of wild animals’ protection and publish the illegal activities.
